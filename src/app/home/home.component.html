About us

This web application was created for powerlifting environment in mind. Main apply of this application is to create very individual training plan to improve weakest lift of the powerlifters.

Frequently asked questions

The training plan generator uses user inputs such as age, bodyweight, bench, deadlift squat and more. Then uses algorithm to compare user results to results of other people and determine which lift is the worst one.The Plan is aimed at the user weakest lift.

The app uses data from the OpenPowerlifting database to compare a user's lift numbers to those of others in their age and weight category. This allows the app to identify which lift may need the most improvement.

The OpenPowerlifting database is a comprehensive collection of powerlifting results from various meets and competitions. It is used to provide users with relevant data and comparisons. This page uses data from the OpenPowerlifting project, https://www.openpowerlifting.org. You may download a copy of the data at https://data.openpowerlifting.org.
